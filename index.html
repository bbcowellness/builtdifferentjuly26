Do I need to be super fit or experienced in the gym?

No. Your plan is built specifically for where your body is right now — including any imbalances, limitations, or joint concerns. We meet you where you are and build from there.

Absolutely not. Faith is woven into the framework of how we think about identity, habits, discipline, and the body. We use Scripture as a lens — not as decoration. If your relationship with God is central to who you are, then it's central to how you train and live.

This was built for real women with real lives — not women with three free hours a day and zero responsibilities. The whole point is to create a sustainable structure that works in your actual life. That said: this does require showing up. Busy is a reality. Unprepared is a choice.
